Ep.3: 社会热点 | AI的日常与思考

【开场】00:00

嗨，你好，Bonjour [bɔ̃.ʒuʁ]! 这里是 Jojo‘s Pod。今天我们来聊聊 AI，有人觉得它是巨大威胁，也有人觉得它能解放我们人类。我想从自己的使用体验出发，来聊聊它的用处，以及它带来的问题和可能性。

Hi there, Bonjour [bɔ̃.ʒuʁ]! This is Jojo’s Pod. Today we’re talking about AI — some see it as a huge threat, others as a force that could liberate humanity. I’d like to start from my own experiences, to explore its uses, its issues, and its possibilities.

【几年间chatGPT基本被闲置】00:54

chatGPT刚刚出现时，我也迫不及待地注册使用……直到今年我再次用回它，明显觉得它进化了好多。

When ChatGPT first appeared, I eagerly signed up…… It wasn’t until this year, when I tried it again, that I felt how much it had evolved.

【阅读助手】01:34

先是发现它在提高阅读效率方面很有用……作为阅读助手，必须说它已经超级棒。

The first big surprise was how much it helped my reading…… But even so, as a reading assistant, it’s fantastic.

【个人护理与陪伴】02:21

随后我也在日常生活中发现它的用处……AI 虽然没有感受，但在信息检索、提醒错误操作与提供心理支持上，确实可以成为一种实用的陪伴。当然，责任依然在人类。

Then I began to see its usefulness in everyday life…… AI doesn’t have feelings, but when it comes to finding info, flagging mistakes, and offering a bit of emotional support, it can actually be a handy companion. Responsibility’s still ours, though.

【工作与创作支持】04:35

后来，它也逐渐进入到我的工作里……AI 的逻辑是条理化、可预测的，而创作却可能需要混乱、偶然和不确定性。如果过度依赖它，把一切都安排得清清楚楚，可能反而会削弱那些意外的火花。因此，人类的主体性更显得重要。

After that, it gradually entered my work as well…… AI’s logic is orderly and predictable, whereas creativity may require chaos, chance, and uncertainty. If you rely on it too much, you might weaken those unexpected sparks. That’s why human agency matters even more.

【封闭于自我的可能性】05:51

除了工作相关内容，我也会与AI讨论一些社会事件……而在社会议题上，因其带来的回音室效应也尤其危险。

Besides work-related matters, I also discuss social events with AI…… And with social issues, the echo chamber effect can be especially dangerous.

【风险与责任】06:40

这种风险是真实存在的……对个人来说，我们要决定如何使用 AI；对社会来说，我们要决定如何监督与规范它。

This risk is real…… On the individual level, we must decide how to use AI; on the societal level, we must decide how to regulate and supervise it.

【判断力训练】07:48

所以在使用 AI 时，要求人类必须有较强的判断力……以这样一种方式，我们可以始终保持思考的开放性，训练判断力的敏锐与流动。

That’s why, when using AI, we’ve got to practice strong judgment…… In this way, we can maintain openness of thought, and cultivate sensitivity and flexibility in our judgment.

【创造力仍在继续】08:41

最后，再说回到艺术与创造力……AI 有各种用途，但真正的作品仍然需要人去做出那一笔、一处肌理、一种选择。

Finally, back to art and creativity…… AI can help in lots of ways, but a real work of art still needs the human touch — that one stroke, that one texture, that one choice.
